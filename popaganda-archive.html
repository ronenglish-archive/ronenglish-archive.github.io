PoPaganda Archive

This site is a long-form public record of Ron English’s PoPaganda practice—focused on exhibitions, murals, installations, collaborations, public actions, and supporting ephemera.

Scope

The PoPaganda Archive is being assembled as a structured timeline and reference index. Entries will be added as documentation is verified and organized into consistent records (dates, locations, participants, media, and sources).

  • Exhibitions and events (solo and group)
  • Murals and public works
  • Brand activations and collaborations
  • Printed ephemera (showcards, flyers, invitations, press materials)
  • Press coverage and published documentation
  • Photographic evidence and installation views
Method

Records will prioritize primary documentation when available (artist documentation, official announcements, exhibition materials, reputable press), with citations and dated sources as the archive expands.

Release plan

Initial public release is scheduled for June 2027, with staged publishing as sections reach a stable, citable state. The Catalogue Raisonné and this archive are designed to work together: the Catalogue centers on artworks, while this archive centers on context, public presentation, and documented history.

Contribute documentation

If you have documentation that strengthens a record (date/location confirmations, installation photos, printed materials, press clippings, or archival scans), you can submit it for review. Submissions are reviewed prior to inclusion in the archive.
